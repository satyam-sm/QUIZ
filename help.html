FAQ

How do I start a quiz?

To begin a quiz, simply navigate to the quiz page and choose the subject. Make sure you're logged in if required.

How do I answer a question?

Once you're inside a quiz, read the question carefully and select your answer choice by clicking on it. Click "Next" to move to the next question.

Can I skip a question and come back to it later?

Yes, you can. Click "Skip" to move to the next question without answering the current one. You can return to skipped questions using the "Previous" button.

Can I retake a quiz?

Some quizzes may allow retakes, while others may not. Check the quiz details for retake options.

What if I encounter technical issues?

If you face any technical problems during a quiz, please contact our support team via abc@gmail.com for assistance.
